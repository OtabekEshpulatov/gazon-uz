Frequently Asked Questions

We know you might have some questions, well here are the answers...

Who are Playfinder?
Playfinder provides a digital marketplace making it easy to book sports facilities online. We also provide a booking system, Bookteq, and a Fully Managed service that can help facility operators fill spare capacity and streamline operations. Playfinder works with operators of all types and sizes, ranging from local community centres and schools to major leisure operators, councils, and governing bodies.
Does Playfinder offer an online booking software?
Playfinder provides an cloud-based online booking software called Bookteq. The booking software not only facilitates online bookings and payments, but also simplifies the management of multiple venues and provides a bespoke invoicing solution which helps collect payments on time.
I already have a booking software. Do I need to replace it?
Playfinder's in-house developers specialise in booking software integrations, meaning there's no requirement to change your current software as long as we can integrate with it. Please contact us to see whether this is possible. All bookings made via these integrations are fully automated and speak directly to your existing booking system, preventing any double bookings and ensuring you are paid directly.
What are the benefits of bringing bookings online?
Customers are 5x time more likely to make a booking if they can view availability and make a booking online. Playfinder’s system allows online bookings and payments to be made 24/7 without the hassle of answering phone calls or emails. A simple booking journey eliminates enquiries for unavailable times, encourages new users, and makes it easy to rebook. As a result, Playfinder partners that offer online booking calendars see a significant increase in bookings at their venue.
What type of bookings can be made on Playfinder?
Playfinder processes a wide range of bookings for our partnered venues including ad hoc bookings, block bookings, events such as birthday parties and corporate days, space hire, and filming sessions.
I need help managing my bookings. Can you help?
Yes we can. As a part of our Fully Managed service, we offer outsourced booking management to our partners. Our dedicated sports booking team will manage all bookings, enquiries, invoices, and reports on your behalf, allowing you to focus on other key areas of your business.
How will my venue be promoted?
Your venue will be featured as a recommended partner venue listing on Playfinder’s online marketplace. Facilities also benefit from professional photography and are included in regular digital marketing campaigns to sports players. Our digital marketing team are experts in maximising venue exposure to increase both player participation and facility utilisation.
Does Playfinder work with schools?
Playfinder and our booking system, Bookteq, have been designed to work with schools, academies, universities and other educational facilities to help open up bookings to local communities, fill spare capacity and increase revenue generated. A feature of our booking system means you can always make sure you approve a customer before their booking is confirmed.
Does Playfinder work with councils?
Playfinder partners with local authorities who have kept some, if not all, facility management in-house. Our booking system, Bookteq, and Fully Managed service is ideal for councils who are looking for ways to manage sports bookings. Councils such as Waltham Forest, Ealing and Bedford all benefit from choosing Playfinder as their bookings partner which has led to a reduction of time spent on sports bookings, increased accessibility by local residents and better usage of the facilities.
Does Playfinder work with sports clubs?
Playfinder has a large and growing number of sports clubs with fantastic facilities, helping to fill spare capacity and providing an exceptional user journey for customers. Our booking system, Bookteq, was built following a pilot with the Football Foundation and we work with a number of clubs funded by the Football Foundation, the LTA, and other sport governing bodies.
Does Playfinder work with leisure centres?
The Playfinder marketplace has integrated with a number of leisure operators and we work hand in hand with them to promote spare capacity and increase the exposure of their facilities. Our experience working with the likes of GLL, Fusion Leisure and Everyone Active means that we can work proactively to achieve the business objectives of our partners and ensure they can be accessed by a large community of recreational sports players.
Is the service GDPR compliant?
Playfinder is fully compliant for GDPR and are advised by data security specialists, making us your perfect GDPR partner. All payments processed are PCI DSS compliant.
